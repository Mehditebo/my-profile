How did I start longboarding ? 🤔

First of all, I have always seeked hobbies that could procure me adrenaline and I remember seeing all new skaters on Instagram doing crazy tricks and dance moves on longboard. This artistic and technical side originally from skateboard make this sport unique so after I graduated from high school in 2017 I decided to perform in Longboarding.

Do I belong to an organisation ? 🗼

Of course ! I have been enjoying my training at the DockSession Paris (Quai anatole France) where I am a member and volunteer. This french association promotes longboard dancing & freestyle across the world for instance by organising competitions or free lessons and I am glad to keep progressing in such good environment.

What am I doing outside of longboarding ? 🤷🏽‍♂️

Well, I am master's student in management at Kedge Business School and precisely in supply chain sector. Then, I would like to be a purchaser.
